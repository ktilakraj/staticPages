Step 2: The Rally (How Your Players Score Points)

  • Ace: +2 pts
  • Break of Serve: +5 pts
  • Double Fault: -1 pt
  • Unforced Error: -0.5 pts

Match Result Bonuses:

  • Win a Set: +3 pts
  • Win in Straight Sets: +10 pts
  • Win the Match: +10 pts

Special Bonuses:

  • Clean Sweep (6–0 Set): +5 pts
  • Underdog Upset: +15 pts
